About us


The MGL brothers specialise in all types of Steel Fixing for vast infrastructure projects including light rails, roadwork and bridges. We are able to cater for all civil, industrial, commercial and residential projects all around Australia. With a large crew of experienced steel fixers ready to take on your project. 

We provide great leading hands to organize and install your steel placements as fast as possible. All our work is quality assured in accordance with Australian Standards. Adaptable to the size of your needs. 

Services

Our company is able to facilitate labor hire, steel fixing, welding and prefabrication to achieve success in all types of residential and commercial projects such as footings, basements, large retaining walls, pools and bridges.
